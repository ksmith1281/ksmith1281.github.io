Magnetic Metal Made Transparent

Metals can be a bastion of magnetic properties, but are often avoided due to high reflection and absorption. I designed and developed RF filters capable of transmission through highly reflective magnetic metals with extremely high magneto-photonic effects. With each design verified and optimized through numerical analysis via MATLAB, I am seasoned with interfacing complex physics with systemic programming.

A Band is a Business

And the fans are the customers.
